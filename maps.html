Maps Strategy Guide

Each map changes how you play, loot, and fight

Erangel

Balanced map with mixed terrain

Best Weapons: ARs, DMRs

Playstyle: Tactical + mid-range fights

Miramar

Desert map with long-range fights

Best Weapons: Snipers, DMRs

Playstyle: Long-distance combat

Sanhok

Small fast-paced jungle map

Best Weapons: SMGs, ARs

Playstyle: Close combat, fast fights

Vikendi

Snow map with mixed combat range

Best Weapons: ARs, Snipers

Playstyle: Balanced survival fights

Rondo

Modern city + open field hybrid map

Best Weapons: ARs, SMGs

Playstyle: Urban + rotation fights

How Maps Connect to Weapons

Bigger maps favor snipers. Smaller maps favor SMGs. Smart players adapt their loadout based on terrain.
